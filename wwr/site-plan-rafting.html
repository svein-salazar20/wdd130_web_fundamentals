Overview

Purpose

The purpose of my website is to show the funny and safe that white water rafting is. We need the website so everyone can reach our services and get everything they need to have fun with us; we want to increase the amount of visitors, we also want show the visitor's experience to new people and have more people visiting the rapids. This will benefit our rafting company by increasing the sales and image of the white water rafting with us.

Audience

our website will be addressed to young people looking for adventure, families that want to have fun togheter, all the people looking for an adventure vacation or extreme activities, the type of person we need to keep in mind as we are building our site is someone that is looking for an activity to do on the weekend of is planning a trip with fun activities to do. Our content, colors, fonts, etc, will be bold, reflecting the xtreme, secure, and fun that white water rafting is, using contrats, and striking elements to hook everyone visiting the homepage through desktop, laptop and movile device.
